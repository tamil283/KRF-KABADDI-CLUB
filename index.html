KRF FULL MEANING KOZHIYUR ROCKY FRIENDS**Kabaddi** is a contact team sport that originated in ancient India. The name "kabaddi" comes from the Tamil word **"kai-pidi"**, which means "to hold hands"—a reference to the defensive formations used in the game. ### Basic Meaning: Kabaddi is a game where players from two teams take turns sending a "raider" into the opposing team’s half of the court. The raider tries to tag as many defenders as possible and return to their own half **without being tackled**, all while chanting "kabaddi, kabaddi" continuously in a single breath. ### Key Elements: * **Teams**: Two teams with 7 players each. * **Raider**: The attacking player who enters the opponent’s side. * **Defenders**: Players who try to stop the raider. * **Tag and return**: The goal is to tag opponents and get back safely. * **No breath holding**: The raider must keep chanting "kabaddi" without inhaling. ### Objective: Score points by: * **Tagging opponents** as a raider. * **Tackling the raider** as defenders. Kabaddi is popular in South Asia (especially India, Bangladesh, Iran, and Pakistan) and is now played internationally, with leagues and tournaments such as the **Pro Kabaddi League** and **Asian Games**.
